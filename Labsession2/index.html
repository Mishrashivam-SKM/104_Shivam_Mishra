David Goggins

David Goggins is a retired Navy SEAL, ultra-endurance athlete, motivational speaker, and author.
He overcame an abusive childhood, obesity, and numerous failures to become one of the toughest and most mentally resilient individuals on the planet. Goggins is known for pushing the boundaries of human endurance and inspiring millions to embrace discomfort and take control of their lives.
